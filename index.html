General skills in game development

The aspects of videogame development in which i focus the most.

Game design

Since the first concepts to the final implementation, I love designing new and original mechanics that take the players to game experiencies they can enjoy the most.

Level design

The aspect that I like the most. For me, the level design reaches its maximum potential when it plays a crucial role in the gameplay, and that's what I try to do always.

System design

Creating and developing systems that can be use in multiple ways is one of the best parts of working in a team alongside the Code Department.

UI/UX design

Making all the decisions in order to give the players the best gamefeel to improve their experience.

3D modeling

I love modeling my own assets so I can get a much more close view of the levels I'm creating. It also makes easier the communication between Art and Design, and that's crucial.

Documentation

Keeping everything under control requires keeping track of all the decisions, mechanics, ideas, tasks, etc. It's also crucial to keep a good communication between all the departments.
